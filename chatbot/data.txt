1. FAQs

Q: How can I reset my password?
A: Go to Account Settings > Security > Reset Password. You’ll receive an email link to set a new one.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, UPI, and Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe).

Q: Can I cancel an order after placing it?
A: Yes, you can cancel an order within 24 hours before it is shipped.

2. Help Docs (Short Guides)

Password Reset Guide:

Click Forgot Password on the login page.

Enter your registered email/phone.

Follow the reset link sent to your email.

Tracking Orders:

Go to My Orders section.

Select the order you want to track.

You’ll see live tracking status (Processing → Shipped → Out for Delivery → Delivered).

3. Product Guide (E-commerce Example)

Return Policy for Electronics:

Electronics can be returned within 7 days if unopened.

Opened products can only be replaced if defective.

Warranty claims must go through the manufacturer.

Clothing Size Guide:

Small = 36 inches chest

Medium = 40 inches chest

Large = 44 inches chest

4. Policies

Refund Policy: Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days to the original payment method.

Shipping Policy: Standard delivery takes 3–5 days. Express delivery takes 1–2 days (extra charges apply).

Privacy Policy: Customer data is encrypted and not shared with third parties without consent.
